Help us improve FreeFEM's codebase

I am Frédéric Hecht, professor at Sorbonne University in Paris and I created FreeFEM with Olivier Pironneau. I released FreeFEM in 1992, a new implementation of MacFEM completely re-written in C++. Twenty-seven years later, we were very proud to announce the release of FreeFEM version 4.

With such a long history, a major code clean-up is now required to make FreeFEM's code easier to understand, maintain and modify. Your donations are necessary to finance this heavy refactoring. On behalf of the dev team, I thank you in advance for your support.

FreeFEM is part of the FSMP, the Paris Foundation for Mathematical Sciences.
